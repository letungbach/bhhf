Data Management Analysis for Bui Huu Hung Foundation

Current Situation

Problem & Gaps

  • Over-reliance on general tools (Google Suite, Slack, Email).
  • Key Gaps: No dedicated systems for:
    • Collection/Inventory Management
    • CRM/Sales
    • Project Management

Data Issues & Bottlenecks

  • Fragmented data & inconsistent naming.
  • Untapped value in existing data.
  • Inefficiencies across core processes.

Strength

Team Openness to New Tech: 5/5 (High)

Phase 1: Build Foundational Tech (High Priority)

Goal: Address critical operational gaps.

Action 1: Implement Collection Management System (CMS)

Examples: Artwork Archive, Artlogic

Integrate and centralize all artwork data, history, and documentation.

Benefit: Centralized Art Data

Action 2: Implement CRM

Examples: HubSpot, Salesforce (Ensure CMS Integration)

Systematically track relationships, manage communications, and enable targeted outreach.

Benefit: Systematic Relationship Tracking

Action 3: Adopt Project Management Tool

Examples: Asana, Trello

Structure project planning, task assignment, progress tracking, and team collaboration.

Benefit: Structured Planning & Collaboration

Phase 2: Leverage AI

Goal: Enhance capabilities using the new tech foundation.

Action 1: AI for Data Search & Tagging

Utilize AI tools to automatically tag, categorize, and enable semantic search across data.

Benefit: Improved Data Organization & Retrieval

Action 2: AI for Content Creation

Use generative AI for drafting emails, summaries, reports (always with human review).

Benefit: Boost Content Creation Efficiency

Action 3: AI for Lead Identification

Implement AI web scraping/NLP workflow to find potential contacts/partners, feeding data into CRM.

Benefit: Identify Potential New Contacts

(Future) AI for Images

Explore visual search, image recognition, and automatic tagging for the collection.

Benefit: Enhanced Visual Discovery
